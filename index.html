About us

This is NTS


Nacka Tomato Society was formed in the year of 1996 by Tull Matsberg. Tull, working as a gardener at the time, realised that there was large interest of tomatoes in the local communities in Nacka and he received request from several individuals in the municipality to host seminars on tomato farming. Hence the Society was formed. The the Society is all about socialising around the Tomato. We gather, we plant, we harvest and we socialise under the motto: ”Sharing is caring”.

Growing

Our events and gatherings are a perfect spot to further deepen your knowledge on how to grow and tend to tomatoes.

2022 Events

Monthly Gathering


Open to members. We meet the first Saturday of each month. The general agenda is ”Meet and eat” combined with a seminar with a topic related to the season. As a member you will get an invite two weeks prior. Membership is free but each gathering has a fee that relates to the seminar and the menu.

Start of Season


Full day seminar with lunch and dinner in the evening. We explore new tomato varieties and learn both new and old tricks on how to get a lot of red gold. Date for 2022 is set to the 23rd of April. Joel Andersson from Restaurant AG will be the event-chef. Register your interest of attending to save a spot.

Harvest Fest


Harvest time! Its all about food, fun and competition. Who has grown the most beautiful tomato and who makes the best ketchup? Full family event with music and games. This years event will take place at Velamsunds Farmhouse. The date is set to the 27th of August. Register your interest of attending to save a spot.
